why noida · why us

noida rewards the patient designer.

noida has the cleanest grid in the ncr — and it shows in the architecture. wide plot lines, generous setbacks, taller ceiling envelopes than gurgaon high-rises, more daylight available per square foot. that creates room to design. it also creates room to over-design.

we treat noida apartments as architectural problems, not finishing exercises. less surface noise. more attention to circulation, light, and material weight. a 3,500 sqft apartment in sector 150 deserves restraint, not maximalism.

questions clients ask · noida

before you start a project.

what areas in noida do you cover?

we cover all of noida and greater noida — sector 150, sector 128, sector 100, sector 50 and 51, jaypee wishtown, jaypee greens, ats, lotus boulevard, prateek, and along the noida expressway and yamuna expressway corridor.

do you work in jaypee wishtown or sector 150?

yes. sector 150 (with the country's largest green belt and developments like ats picturesque reprieves, ats pious, and lotus boulevard espacia) is one of our most active noida markets. we also work in jaypee wishtown and the broader jaypee greens master plan.

how much does interior design cost in noida?

for noida apartments, our full-scope interior design and execution work typically starts at ₹4,000 per sqft. premium and large-format projects with custom joinery and imported materials range from ₹6,500 to ₹11,000+ per sqft.

do you also design restaurants and commercial spaces in noida?

yes. our hospitality work includes restaurant interior design in noida — including the hashtag restaurant in noida. we work on cafés, restaurants, retail fit-outs, and corporate offices in addition to residential.

do you provide drawings, vendor coordination, and execution?

yes. our scope is end-to-end. design, working drawings, vendor coordination, custom furniture, joinery, lighting, site supervision, and handover. we do not exit at the drawing phase.

what is the typical timeline for a noida apartment?

design phase is 6 to 10 weeks. execution for a 2,000 to 3,500 sqft apartment is typically 4 to 7 months, depending on custom joinery, imported lead times, and society constraints.
