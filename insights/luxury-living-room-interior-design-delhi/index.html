luxury living room interior design delhi: a case study

our client, a young family, had recently moved into a beautiful villa on Golf Course Road, Gurgaon, and were looking to design their living room. they wanted a space that was not only luxurious but also functional and comfortable. after researching online and visiting several design studios, they decided to consult with indéva studio to bring their vision to life.

the brief

the client's brief was to design a luxury living room that would serve as the heart of their home. they wanted a space that would be perfect for relaxing, entertaining, and spending quality time with family and friends. the room had to be designed in a way that would maximize the use of natural light and take advantage of the stunning views of the golf course. the client had a budget of ₹15-25 lakh and wanted the project to be completed within 6-8 weeks.

the challenge

the biggest challenge we faced was the unusual shape of the room. it was a long, narrow space with a sloping ceiling, which made it difficult to design a functional and aesthetically pleasing space. additionally, the client had a lot of existing furniture pieces that they wanted to incorporate into the design, which added to the complexity of the project.

understanding the client's needs

to overcome these challenges, we spent a lot of time understanding the client's needs and preferences. we asked them about their lifestyle, how they planned to use the space, and what kind of atmosphere they wanted to create. we also took detailed measurements of the room and created a 3d model to help us visualize the space and identify potential design opportunities.

the design solution

based on our understanding of the client's needs, we proposed a design solution that would create a sense of warmth and intimacy in the space. we suggested using a combination of warm, earthy tones and rich textures to create a cozy atmosphere. we also recommended using floor-to-ceiling curtains to maximize the natural light and take advantage of the views.

  • we used a combination of wood and stone to add warmth and texture to the space
  • we designed a custom sectional sofa to fit the unusual shape of the room
  • we incorporated the client's existing furniture pieces into the design to add a personal touch
the key to designing a luxury living room is to create a sense of balance and harmony. it's not just about using expensive materials or designer furniture, but about creating a space that feels comfortable, inviting, and reflective of the client's personality.

the result

the final result was a stunning luxury living room that exceeded the client's expectations. the space was warm, inviting, and perfect for relaxing and entertaining. the client was thrilled with the final result and appreciated the attention to detail and commitment to quality that indéva studio brought to the project. the total cost of the project was ₹20 lakh, which was within the client's budget.

frequently asked questions

what is the typical budget for a luxury living room interior design project in delhi?

the typical budget for a luxury living room interior design project in delhi can range from ₹10 lakh to ₹50 lakh or more, depending on the size of the space, the materials used, and the complexity of the design.

how long does it take to complete a luxury living room interior design project?

the timeline for a luxury living room interior design project can vary depending on the scope of the project, but typically ranges from 6-12 weeks.

what is the importance of natural light in a luxury living room design?

natural light is essential in a luxury living room design as it can make the space feel more welcoming, warm, and inviting. it can also help to reduce the need for artificial lighting and create a sense of connection to the outdoors.
