wardrobe design ideas for bedroom india: a warning guide

The day a client called us mid-project, frantic about their wardrobe design, was a stark reminder of the costly mistakes that can happen when designing a bedroom in a heritage bungalow in Lutyens Delhi, Delhi. With a budget of ₹8-15 lakh, our client had envisioned a spacious and elegant bedroom, but their wardrobe design was quickly becoming a nightmare. As a senior writer for indéva studio, i have seen many such disasters, and i want to share some hard-won wisdom on how to avoid them.

mistake 1: inadequate storage planning

what goes wrong: a wardrobe with insufficient storage space can lead to clutter and disorganization, making it difficult to find what you need. why it happens: many homeowners underestimate the amount of storage they need, or they prioritize style over function. exact fix: to avoid this mistake, it's essential to assess your storage needs carefully and plan your wardrobe design accordingly. consider the types of clothing and accessories you have, and allocate sufficient space for each category.

tips for effective storage planning

  • measure your clothing and accessories to determine the optimal shelf and rod height
  • consider using dividers or baskets to separate and organize items
  • don't forget to include storage for shoes, accessories, and other miscellaneous items

mistake 2: poor material selection

what goes wrong: using low-quality materials can result in a wardrobe that is prone to damage and wear and tear. why it happens: many homeowners are tempted to cut costs by using cheap materials, but this can end up being a false economy. exact fix: to avoid this mistake, it's essential to choose high-quality materials that are durable and long-lasting. consider using eco-friendly materials, such as those certified by the indian green building council, to reduce your environmental impact.

a well-designed wardrobe is not just about aesthetics; it's also about functionality and sustainability. by choosing the right materials, you can create a wardrobe that is both beautiful and environmentally friendly.

mistake 3: insufficient lighting

what goes wrong: a wardrobe with inadequate lighting can be difficult to navigate, making it hard to find what you need. why it happens: many homeowners overlook the importance of lighting in their wardrobe design. exact fix: to avoid this mistake, it's essential to include sufficient lighting in your wardrobe design. consider using a combination of overhead lighting, task lighting, and accent lighting to create a well-lit and functional space.

mistake 4: lack of customization

what goes wrong: a wardrobe that is not tailored to your specific needs can be frustrating to use. why it happens: many homeowners opt for a standard wardrobe design, without considering their individual requirements. exact fix: to avoid this mistake, it's essential to work with a designer who can create a customized wardrobe design that meets your unique needs. at indéva studio, we offer bespoke wardrobe design services, with prices starting from ₹8 lakh, depending on the complexity of the design and the materials used.
