what drives costs up in false ceiling designs

several factors contribute to the escalating costs of false ceiling designs, including the type of material used, design complexity, and labor costs. for instance, a simple false ceiling design using pop material can cost between ₹50-₹100 per square foot, while a more intricate design using gypsum or wood can cost upwards of ₹200-₹500 per square foot.
