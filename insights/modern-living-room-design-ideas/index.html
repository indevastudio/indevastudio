modern living room design ideas india 2025: a case study

in the heart of delhi, a young family was looking to renovate their duplex in Greater Kailash, with a budget of ₹2.5 crore. they wanted a modern living room that would be perfect for entertaining guests and spending quality time with their loved ones. our team at indéva studio was excited to take on the challenge and create a space that would exceed their expectations.

the brief

the client's brief was clear: they wanted a living room that was modern, sleek, and functional. they had a clear vision of what they wanted, but needed our expertise to bring it to life. we started by understanding their lifestyle, preferences, and requirements. we spent several hours discussing their needs, from the type of furniture they liked to the kind of lighting they preferred.

the challenge

the biggest challenge we faced was the limited space available. the living room was relatively small, and we had to work within a tight budget of ₹1.5 crore for the living room alone. we also had to ensure that the design was cohesive with the rest of the duplex, which had a more traditional aesthetic. our team had to think creatively to come up with a design that would meet all these requirements.

space planning

we started by creating a detailed floor plan of the living room. we identified the different zones within the room, including a seating area, a dining area, and a bar. we then worked on optimizing the layout to make the most of the available space. we used a combination of built-in furniture and multi-functional pieces to create a sense of flow and visual interest.

  • we used a sleek, low-profile sofa to create a sense of openness
  • we incorporated a built-in bar with a quartz countertop and LED lighting
  • we added a statement piece of art above the sofa to create a focal point

the design solution

our design solution was centered around creating a sense of flow and visual interest. we used a combination of materials, including wood, metal, and glass, to add depth and texture to the space. we also incorporated plenty of greenery to bring in a natural element and purify the air. the result was a living room that was not only beautiful but also functional and sustainable.

the key to a successful design is not just about creating a beautiful space, but also about understanding the client's needs and preferences. in this case, our client wanted a space that was modern, sleek, and functional, and we were able to deliver that while staying within their budget of ₹2.2 crore.

the result

the final result was a living room that exceeded our client's expectations. the space was modern, sleek, and functional, with plenty of natural light and ventilation. the client was thrilled with the outcome, and we were happy to have been able to deliver a space that met their needs and exceeded their expectations. the total cost of the project came in at ₹2.1 crore, which was within the client's budget.
