luxury home interior design cost delhi 2025: mistakes to avoid

the day a client called us mid-project, frustrated and overwhelmed with the skyrocketing costs of their farmhouse interior design in kundli, sonipat, is a day that still resonates with our team at indéva studio. their initial budget of ₹2 crore had already been exceeded by ₹50 lakh, and they were struggling to see the light at the end of the tunnel. as experienced designers, we've seen this story unfold time and time again, which is why we're sharing our expertise to help you avoid the same pitfalls.

not planning for hidden costs

one of the most significant mistakes our client made was not accounting for hidden costs, such as electrical and plumbing work, which can quickly add up to ₹10-20 lakh. this is especially true for farmhouses in kundli, sonipat, where the infrastructure may require additional work.

why it happens

homeowners often underestimate the complexity of luxury interior design projects, which can lead to unexpected expenses. to avoid this, it's essential to work with a reputable design firm like indéva studio, who can provide a comprehensive breakdown of costs and ensure that all aspects of the project are considered.

insufficient research and planning

another critical mistake is not conducting thorough research and planning before embarking on a luxury interior design project. this can result in a disjointed and unfunctional space, which can be costly to rectify.

  • not defining a clear design style and vision
  • not creating a detailed budget and timeline
  • not selecting materials and finishes carefully
it's not just about creating a beautiful space, but also about ensuring that it functions seamlessly and meets the needs of the occupants. as one of our designers always says, "a well-designed space is not just aesthetically pleasing, but also highly functional and efficient."

not working with a reputable design firm

working with a reputable design firm like indéva studio can make all the difference in the success of your luxury interior design project. we have years of experience in designing and executing high-end projects, including farmhouses in kundli, sonipat, and can provide valuable guidance and expertise to ensure that your project stays on track and within budget.

exact fix

to avoid the pitfalls of working with an inexperienced design firm, it's essential to do your research and select a firm that has a proven track record of delivering high-quality projects on time and within budget. you can start by visiting websites like houzz india to find inspiration and get in touch with reputable design firms.

not prioritizing sustainability and energy efficiency

luxury interior design is not just about creating a beautiful space, but also about ensuring that it is sustainable and energy efficient. this can be achieved by incorporating eco-friendly materials, optimizing natural light, and using energy-efficient systems.

not budgeting for smart home technology

with the increasing popularity of smart home technology, it's essential to budget for these features, which can range from ₹5-10 lakh, depending on the complexity of the system. our team at indéva studio can help you navigate the options and create a customized solution that meets your needs and budget.
