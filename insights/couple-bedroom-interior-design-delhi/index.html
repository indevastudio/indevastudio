couple bedroom interior design delhi: a cost guide

did you know that most indians overspend on their bedroom interior design by at least ₹5 lakh? this is often due to a lack of planning and unrealistic expectations. as a senior writer for indéva studio, i've seen many clients struggle to stay within their budget. in this article, we'll explore the biggest cost misconceptions in india and provide a data-driven guide to help you plan your dream bedroom within a budget of ₹25-50 lakh for a villa in golf course road, gurgaon.

what drives costs up in couple bedroom interior design

several factors contribute to the high cost of bedroom interior design, including the choice of materials, furniture, and lighting. for example, a high-end wardrobe can cost anywhere from ₹1.5 lakh to ₹5 lakh, while a luxury bed can range from ₹50,000 to ₹2 lakh. additionally, the cost of labor and installation can add up quickly, with prices starting from ₹500 per square foot.

cost breakdown by room

to give you a better idea of the costs involved, let's break down the estimated expenses for a couple bedroom in a villa on golf course road, gurgaon:

  • wardrobe: ₹1.5 lakh - ₹5 lakh
  • bed: ₹50,000 - ₹2 lakh
  • lighting: ₹20,000 - ₹1 lakh
  • flooring: ₹50,000 - ₹2 lakh
  • walls and ceilings: ₹30,000 - ₹1.5 lakh

tips for reducing costs

one way to reduce costs is to opt for more affordable materials and designs. for example, you can choose a mid-range wardrobe for ₹80,000 instead of a high-end one for ₹5 lakh. you can also consider shopping during sales or using second-hand items to save money.

according to architectural digest india, the key to affordable interior design is to strike a balance between form and function. by prioritizing your needs and being mindful of your budget, you can create a beautiful and functional space without breaking the bank.

how to negotiate with your interior designer

when working with an interior designer, it's essential to communicate your budget and expectations clearly. you can ask your designer to provide a detailed breakdown of the costs and suggest ways to reduce expenses. at indéva studio, we offer our services to help you plan and execute your interior design project within your budget.

red flags that inflate bills

there are several red flags to watch out for when it comes to interior design costs, including:

  • hidden charges: make sure to ask your designer about any additional fees or charges.
  • low-quality materials: be wary of designers who suggest using low-quality materials to cut costs.
  • unrealistic timelines: be skeptical of designers who promise to complete the project in an unrealistically short timeframe.

real project budget example

let's take a look at a real project budget example for a couple bedroom in a villa on golf course road, gurgaon. with a budget of ₹35 lakh, we were able to design and execute a beautiful and functional space that met the client's needs and expectations. the breakdown of the costs was as follows:

  • bed: ₹1 lakh
  • lighting: ₹50,000
