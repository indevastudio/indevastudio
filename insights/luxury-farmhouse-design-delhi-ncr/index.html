luxury farmhouse design delhi ncr: a case study

in a quiet corner of lutyens delhi, delhi, stood a beautiful heritage bungalow, waiting to be rediscovered. our client, a family of four, had purchased this stunning property with the vision of turning it into their dream luxury farmhouse. with a budget of ₹25–50 lakh, they approached indéva studio to bring their vision to life.

the brief

our client's brief was clear: they wanted to retain the original charm of the heritage bungalow while incorporating modern luxury farmhouse design elements. they envisioned a space that would serve as a retreat from the chaos of city life, with ample natural light, ventilation, and a strong connection to the outdoors. the project timeline was set at 12 weeks, and our team was ready to take on the challenge.

understanding the client's needs

we began by understanding our client's lifestyle, preferences, and requirements. we learned that they were a family who valued simplicity, sustainability, and comfort. they wanted their luxury farmhouse to reflect these values while also showcasing their personal style. with this insight, we set out to create a design that would not only meet but exceed their expectations.

the challenge

the heritage bungalow, although beautiful, presented several challenges. the structure was old, and the layout was not conducive to modern luxury farmhouse design. the rooms were small, and the natural light was limited. additionally, the client's budget of ₹25–50 lakh was relatively modest compared to the scope of the project. our team had to be creative and resourceful to overcome these challenges.

overcoming budget constraints

to stay within the budget, we decided to focus on the essential elements of the design. we prioritized the use of local, sustainable materials and opted for a minimalist approach to decor. we also encouraged our client to reuse and repurpose existing elements, such as the original wooden doors and windows, to add character to the space.

  • reused existing wooden doors and windows: ₹50,000
  • local, sustainable materials for construction: ₹8,00,000
  • minimalist decor: ₹2,00,000

the design solution

our design solution was centered around creating a sense of flow and continuity throughout the luxury farmhouse. we opened up the small rooms to create larger, more airy spaces, and installed large windows and doors to bring in natural light and ventilation. we also incorporated sustainable design elements, such as a rainwater harvesting system and a solar panel array, to reduce the carbon footprint of the property.

the key to successful luxury farmhouse design is not just about creating a beautiful space, but also about creating a sense of connection to the natural world. by incorporating sustainable design elements and using local materials, we can create spaces that are not only stunning but also environmentally responsible.

selecting materials and finishes

we selected materials and finishes that were not only aesthetically pleasing but also durable and low-maintenance. we used a combination of natural stone, wood, and earthy tones to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. the total cost of materials and finishes came out to be ₹15,00,000, which was well within our client's budget.

the result

the final result was a stunning luxury farmhouse that exceeded our client's expectations. the space was light, airy, and filled with natural beauty. the sustainable design elements and local materials added a sense of authenticity and connection to the natural world. our client was thrilled with the outcome, and we were proud to have delivered a project that met their vision and budget.

the total cost of the project came out to be ₹35,00,000, which was within our client's budget of ₹25–50 lakh. we were able to achieve this by being mindful of our client's budget and making conscious design decisions throughout the project.
