modern luxury with an indian twist

this design direction combines the best of modern luxury with traditional indian elements, creating a unique and sophisticated look. think marble floors, wooden ceilings, and silk upholstery, paired with modern furniture pieces and sleek lighting fixtures. the cost range for this style can vary from ₹30 lakh to ₹45 lakh, depending on the materials and complexity of the design. it suits those who appreciate the fusion of different styles and want to create a one-of-a-kind space.

minimalist chic

for those who prefer a more understated approach to luxury, minimalist chic is a great option. this design direction emphasizes clean lines, simple shapes, and a limited color palette, creating a calm and serene atmosphere. think white or light-colored walls, minimalist furniture, and plenty of natural light. the cost range for this style can vary from ₹25 lakh to ₹40 lakh, depending on the materials and complexity of the design. it suits those who value simplicity and want to create a peaceful living space.

the key to achieving minimalist chic is to focus on a few statement pieces and keep the rest of the space simple and uncluttered. this allows the eye to move freely and creates a sense of calm and serenity.

traditional indian luxury

for those who want to incorporate traditional indian elements into their design, this direction is a great option. think intricate carvings, ornate patterns, and rich materials like wood and silk. the cost range for this style can vary from ₹35 lakh to ₹50 lakh, depending on the materials and complexity of the design. it suits those who appreciate traditional indian aesthetics and want to create a unique and culturally significant space.
