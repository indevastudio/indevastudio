luxury restaurant interior design in gurgaon: curated ideas

imagine stepping into a luxurious restaurant in dlf phase 5, gurgaon, where the ambiance is as exquisite as the cuisine. the space is filled with natural light, and the interior design is a perfect blend of modern and traditional indian elements. the walls are adorned with intricate wooden carvings, and the floors are made of polished marble. the furniture is sleek and modern, with a hint of indian craftsmanship.

modern minimalism

for a modern minimalist design, consider using materials like glass, steel, and concrete. the cost range for this style can be around ₹30-40 lakh. this design suits those who appreciate clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and an emphasis on functionality. some key features of this style include:

  • monochromatic color scheme
  • geometric patterns
  • minimal furniture

traditional indian luxury

for a traditional indian luxury design, consider using materials like wood, stone, and intricate carvings. the cost range for this style can be around ₹40-50 lakh. this design suits those who appreciate rich, opulent interiors and a strong sense of indian heritage. some key features of this style include:

the use of traditional indian motifs, such as the lotus flower or the peacock, can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to the design

using indian textiles

indian textiles, such as silk and cotton, can add a pop of color and texture to the design. consider using them for upholstery, rugs, or even wall hangings. the cost range for indian textiles can be around ₹5-10 lakh.

fusion design

for a fusion design, consider combining modern and traditional elements. the cost range for this style can be around ₹25-35 lakh. this design suits those who appreciate a unique, eclectic aesthetic. some key features of this style include:

  • mixing modern and traditional materials
  • combining geometric and organic patterns
  • incorporating indian craftsmanship with modern techniques
