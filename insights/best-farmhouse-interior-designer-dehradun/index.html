designing your dream dehradun farmhouse

imagine a luxurious villa on golf course road, gurgaon, with a budget of ₹40–80 lakh - the perfect blend of modernity and traditional indian aesthetics, where every detail is carefully crafted to create a unique and inviting space, from the intricate wooden carvings to the vibrant colors of the local textiles, all coming together to evoke the warmth and hospitality of indian culture.

modern farmhouse design

a modern farmhouse design is all about clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and an emphasis on functionality, with materials such as steel, glass, and concrete, and a cost range of ₹50-70 lakh, suitable for those who value simplicity and efficiency, and are looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish space, with plenty of natural light and ventilation, and a strong connection to the outdoors.

key elements

  • large windows and sliding glass doors
  • open-plan living areas
  • sleek and modern furnishings
the key to a successful modern farmhouse design is to strike a balance between form and function, creating a space that is both beautiful and functional, and that reflects the owner's personality and lifestyle.

traditional indian farmhouse design

a traditional indian farmhouse design is all about embracing the rich cultural heritage of the country, with intricate carvings, vibrant colors, and ornate details, and a cost range of ₹40-60 lakh, suitable for those who value tradition and authenticity, and are looking for a space that reflects their roots and cultural identity, with elements such as wooden beams, brick walls, and traditional indian furnishings.

key elements

  • intricate wooden carvings and ornate details
  • traditional indian furnishings and textiles
  • use of natural materials such as wood, brick, and stone

eclectic farmhouse design

an eclectic farmhouse design is all about mixing and matching different styles and elements to create a unique and personalized space, with a cost range of ₹60-80 lakh, suitable for those who value creativity and self-expression, and are looking for a space that reflects their individuality and personality, with elements such as vintage furnishings, colorful textiles, and unusual decorative pieces.

key elements

  • mismatched furnishings and decorative pieces
  • bold and vibrant colors
  • unusual materials and textures

sustainable farmhouse design

a sustainable farmhouse design is all about reducing the environmental impact of the space, with elements such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and eco-friendly materials, and a cost range of ₹50-70 lakh, suitable for those who value the environment and are looking for a space that is not only beautiful but also sustainable, with a focus on energy efficiency and minimal waste.

key elements

  • solar panels and renewable energy systems
  • rainwater harvesting and greywater reuse
  • eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices
