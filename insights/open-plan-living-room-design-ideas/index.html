open plan living room design india: a curated collection of luxury ideas

imagine walking into a spacious, sun-drenched open plan living room in a luxurious villa on Golf Course Road, Gurgaon, with floor-to-ceiling windows that seamlessly blend the indoors with the outdoors. the room is filled with an abundance of natural light, and the air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flowers. the interior design is a perfect blend of modern and traditional, with sleek lines, elegant furnishings, and intricate indian patterns. this is the epitome of luxury living in india, and it can be yours within a budget of ₹40-80 lakh.

modern minimalist open plan living room design

a modern minimalist open plan living room design is perfect for those who appreciate clean lines, simplicity, and functionality. this design style features a neutral color palette, sleek furniture, and ample storage space. the materials used are often industrial-chic, such as concrete, steel, and glass. the cost range for this design style can vary from ₹50-70 lakh, depending on the specific materials and furnishings chosen. this design style suits young couples and families who value simplicity and ease of maintenance.

traditional indian open plan living room design

a traditional indian open plan living room design is perfect for those who appreciate the rich cultural heritage of india. this design style features intricate patterns, ornate furnishings, and vibrant colors. the materials used are often traditional indian materials, such as wood, stone, and textiles. the cost range for this design style can vary from ₹40-60 lakh, depending on the specific materials and furnishings chosen. this design style suits families who value tradition and cultural heritage.

incorporating indian aesthetics

to incorporate indian aesthetics into your open plan living room design, consider using traditional indian materials, such as wood and stone, and incorporating intricate patterns and ornate furnishings. you can also add indian-inspired accessories, such as intricate textiles and colorful rugs. some popular indian aesthetics to consider include:

  • jaali work: intricate stone or wood carvings that add a touch of elegance to any room
  • bandhani: a traditional indian textile technique that involves tying and dyeing fabric to create intricate patterns
  • warli art: a traditional indian art form that involves creating intricate patterns and designs using natural materials
the key to incorporating indian aesthetics into your open plan living room design is to balance traditional elements with modern functionality and simplicity. by doing so, you can create a space that is both culturally rich and aesthetically pleasing.

luxury open plan living room design with a twist

a luxury open plan living room design with a twist is perfect for those who want to add a unique touch to their space. this design style features a bold color palette, eclectic furnishings, and statement pieces. the materials used are often luxurious, such as marble, velvet, and gold. the cost range for this design style can vary from ₹60-80 lakh, depending on the specific materials and furnishings chosen. this design style suits families who value individuality and self-expression.

sustainable open plan living room design

a sustainable open plan living room design is perfect for those who value eco-friendliness and environmental responsibility. this design style features natural materials, energy-efficient lighting, and eco-friendly furnishings. the materials used are often sustainable, such as reclaimed wood, bamboo, and low-voc paints. the cost range for this design style can vary from ₹40-60 lakh, depending on the specific materials and furnishings chosen. this design style suits families who value sustainability and environmental responsibility.
