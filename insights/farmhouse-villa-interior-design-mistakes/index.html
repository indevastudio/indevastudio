farmhouse villa interior design gurgaon: a warning guide

the day a client called us mid-project, frantic about their heritage bungalow in lutyens delhi, delhi, was the day we realized the importance of careful planning in farmhouse villa interior design. with a budget of ₹25-50 lakh, every mistake counts, and we had to act fast to get their project back on track.

not defining the design brief

what goes wrong when you don't define a clear design brief is that the project can quickly go off-track, leading to costly changes and delays. this happens because without a clear understanding of the client's needs and expectations, the design team can't create a cohesive and functional design. to fix this, it's essential to work closely with your designer to create a detailed design brief that outlines your goals, budget, and timeline.

the importance of a clear brief

a clear brief helps to ensure that everyone involved in the project is on the same page, reducing the risk of miscommunication and mistakes. it also helps to establish a clear direction for the design, making it easier to make decisions and stay within budget.

ignoring sustainability

ignoring sustainability in your farmhouse villa interior design can lead to a range of problems, from increased energy costs to compromised indoor air quality. this happens because sustainable design is often seen as an afterthought, rather than an integral part of the design process. to fix this, it's essential to work with a designer who is experienced in sustainable design and can help you create a space that is not only beautiful but also environmentally friendly.

as the indian green building council notes, sustainable design is no longer a luxury, but a necessity, and it's essential to incorporate sustainable principles into your design from the outset.

not budgeting for contingencies

not budgeting for contingencies is a common mistake that can quickly blow your budget. this happens because unforeseen expenses can arise at any time, and without a contingency fund, you can be left scrambling to find the extra funds. to fix this, it's essential to budget for contingencies from the outset, setting aside a portion of your budget for unexpected expenses.

  • set aside 10-20% of your budget for contingencies
  • review your budget regularly to ensure you're on track
  • be prepared to make adjustments as needed

hiring the wrong designer

hiring the wrong designer can be a costly mistake, both in terms of time and money. this happens because the wrong designer may not have the necessary experience or expertise to bring your vision to life. to fix this, it's essential to do your research and find a designer who is experienced in farmhouse villa interior design and has a portfolio that reflects your style and budget.
