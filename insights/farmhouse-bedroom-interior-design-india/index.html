farmhouse bedroom interior design india: a case study

in a bustling city like noida, it's rare to find a peaceful retreat that combines luxury and comfort. our client, a young couple, had just purchased a builder floor in sector 44, noida, and were looking to design their dream farmhouse bedroom. with a budget of ₹8-15 lakh, they approached indéva studio to bring their vision to life.

the brief

the client's brief was clear: they wanted a luxurious and cozy farmhouse bedroom that reflected their personal style. they provided us with a mood board, featuring earthy tones, natural textures, and vintage elements. our team at indéva studio was excited to take on the challenge and create a unique space that met the client's expectations.

the challenge

the biggest challenge we faced was working within the given budget of ₹8-15 lakh. we had to be mindful of every design decision, ensuring that it not only met the client's vision but also fit within the allocated budget. another challenge was the limited natural light in the room, which meant we had to rely on artificial lighting to create a warm and inviting ambiance.

design decisions

to overcome the challenges, we made several key design decisions. we opted for a mix of high-end and affordable materials, such as reclaimed wood and vintage furniture, to add character to the space. we also decided to use led lighting to create a warm glow, which not only saved energy but also fit within the budget. some of the specific design elements we incorporated include:

  • reclaimed wood flooring, sourced from a local supplier, which added a touch of warmth and character to the space
  • vintage furniture pieces, such as a distressed wooden dresser and a plush armchair, which added a layer of sophistication
  • earthy tone walls, painted with a combination of natural paints and textures, which created a cozy and inviting atmosphere
the key to creating a luxurious space on a budget is to focus on a few statement pieces and balance them with more affordable elements. in this case, the reclaimed wood flooring and vintage furniture pieces were the stars of the show, while the earthy tone walls provided a subtle backdrop.

the design solution

our design solution was a direct response to the client's brief and the challenges we faced. we created a space that was not only luxurious and cozy but also reflective of the client's personal style. the room featured a plush bed with a reclaimed wood headboard, a vintage dresser, and a comfortable reading nook with a plush armchair. the earthy tone walls and led lighting added warmth and depth to the space, creating a cozy retreat that felt like a true escape from the city.

the result

the final result was a farmhouse bedroom that exceeded the client's expectations. the space was not only beautiful but also functional, with plenty of storage and a comfortable reading nook. the client was thrilled with the outcome, and we were proud to have delivered a project that met their vision and budget. the final cost of the project came in at ₹12 lakh, which was well within the client's budget of ₹8-15 lakh.
