Home Salon Setup Cost India 2025: Luxury Design Guide

13 April 2026 · By Indéva Studio

Home Salon Setup Cost India 2025: Your Luxury Sanctuary Awaits

Imagine stepping into your personal oasis, a space where the world's demands fade away, replaced by the soothing touch of expert care. For many, the dream of a home salon in India is about more than just convenience; it's about reclaiming a slice of luxury, a private retreat designed for ultimate rejuvenation. But as you envision this tranquil haven, a practical question arises: what is the realistic home salon setup cost in India 2025? At Indéva Studio, we understand that creating such a space is an investment in your well-being, and we're here to guide you through the nuances of its cost, ensuring your vision translates into a beautiful, functional reality.

Defining Your Home Salon Vision: From Minimalist Chic to Opulent Retreat

The journey to understanding your home salon setup cost begins with a clear vision. Are you picturing a compact corner dedicated to quick beauty rituals, or a comprehensive suite offering a full spectrum of spa-like experiences? Your aspirations will significantly shape the investment required.

Essential Elements vs. Bespoke Luxury

A minimalist setup might focus on core functionalities – a comfortable chair, good lighting, and essential storage for products. Conversely, a more elaborate home salon could include:

  • Dedicated treatment beds
  • Advanced aesthetic equipment
  • A separate area for manicures and pedicures
  • A relaxation lounge
  • Soundproofing for enhanced tranquility
  • Custom cabinetry and bespoke finishes

Consider the services you anticipate offering. Will it be primarily for personal use, or will you occasionally host friends or even a professional stylist? The scope of your intended use will dictate the scale of your setup.

The Pillars of Home Salon Setup Cost in India 2025

Understanding the cost involves breaking it down into key components. These are the fundamental building blocks that contribute to the overall investment in your home salon.

1. Space Allocation and Renovation

The most significant factor influencing cost is the space you dedicate. Even a small room requires thoughtful design to be functional and aesthetically pleasing. If you're converting an existing room, consider:

  • Flooring: Opting for durable, easy-to-clean, and aesthetically pleasing materials. Options range from premium vinyl and engineered wood to marble or granite for a truly luxurious feel.
  • Wall Treatments: Paint, designer wallpapers, or even textured finishes can transform a space. Consider moisture-resistant options for areas like mani-pedi stations.
  • Lighting: Crucial for any salon. This includes ambient, task, and accent lighting. Integrated smart lighting systems can add a modern touch.
  • Plumbing & Electrical: If you require a dedicated sink or specialized electrical outlets for equipment, this will add to the renovation cost.

Estimated Cost: INR 1,50,000 - INR 8,00,000+ depending on the size of the space and the extent of renovation. For a high-end, custom-designed space in Delhi NCR, this could easily reach INR 10,00,000 or more.

2. Essential Furniture and Fixtures

Comfort, functionality, and style are paramount when selecting furniture. These pieces are the workhorses of your salon.

a) Seating & Treatment Areas

  • Styling Chairs: Invest in ergonomic and comfortable chairs that can swivel and adjust.
  • Treatment Beds: For facials, massages, or waxing, a high-quality, adjustable bed is essential.
  • Manicure Tables: Ensure adequate workspace, built-in ventilation, and comfortable seating for both the technician and client.
  • Pedicure Stations: These can range from simple foot baths to integrated spa chairs with massage functions.

b) Storage Solutions

  • Cabinets and Shelving: To keep products, towels, and equipment organised and out of sight. Custom-built units offer a seamless look.
  • Trolleys and Carts: For easy mobility of tools and products during treatments.

Estimated Cost: INR 1,00,000 - INR 5,00,000. High-end, custom-designed furniture can significantly increase this figure.

3. Professional Equipment and Tools

This is where the "salon" aspect truly comes to life. The quality and type of equipment will directly impact the services you can offer and the client experience.

a) Hair Care and Styling

  • Professional Hair Dryers & Styling Tools (straighteners, curling irons)
  • Hair Steamer
  • Sanitisation Equipment (UV sterilizers)

b) Skincare and Facials

  • Facial Steamer
  • Magnifying Lamp
  • High-Frequency Machine
  • Microdermabrasion Machine (optional, for advanced treatments)

c) Nail Care

  • Nail Lamps (UV/LED)
  • Electric Nail Files
  • Sterilizers

d) General Salon Equipment

  • Towels and Linens
  • Sterilisation and Sanitation Supplies
  • Waste Disposal Bins

Estimated Cost: INR 50,000 - INR 3,00,000+. The range is vast depending on whether you opt for professional-grade equipment or more consumer-oriented versions.

4. Aesthetics and Ambiance: The Indéva Studio Touch

Beyond functionality, the atmosphere of your home salon is paramount. It’s the element that elevates a mere room into a sanctuary. This includes:

a) Colour Palette and Finishes

Choosing calming colours, luxurious textures, and sophisticated finishes sets the tone. Think serene blues, gentle greens, warm neutrals, or rich jewel tones, complemented by materials like brushed gold, marble accents, or deep wood grains.

b) Décor and Accessories

  • Art and Wall Hangings
  • Plants to enhance the natural feel
  • Aromatherapy Diffusers and high-quality essential oils
  • Soft Furnishings (cushions, throws)
  • Mirrors – not just functional, but statement pieces

c) Sound and Scent

A curated playlist of calming music and subtle, pleasant scents contribute significantly to the spa-like experience. Investing in a good sound system and high-quality diffusers can make a world of difference.

Estimated Cost: INR 50,000 - INR 2,00,000+. This is where personal taste and bespoke design choices come into play.

Calculating Your Total Home Salon Setup Cost in India 2025

Let's bring it all together. The home salon setup cost in India 2025 can vary dramatically based on your choices. Here are some indicative ranges:

  • Basic & Functional (INR 3,00,000 - INR 6,00,000): This would involve a smaller dedicated space, essential yet good-quality furniture and equipment, and minimal renovation. Focus is on utility and cleanliness.
  • Comfortable & Stylish (INR 6,00,000 - INR 12,00,000): This tier includes more significant renovation, better quality furniture, a wider range of professional equipment, and thoughtful decorative touches for a pleasant ambiance.
  • Luxury & Bespoke (INR 12,00,000 - INR 25,00,000+): This is for those who desire a truly premium experience. It encompasses extensive renovations, custom-designed furniture, top-tier professional equipment, advanced lighting and sound systems, and exquisite décor, creating a sanctuary that rivals high-end commercial salons.

These figures are estimations for the Delhi NCR region and can vary based on location, material choices, and the specific designers or contractors engaged. Remember, this is an investment in your personal well-being and a potential enhancement to your home's value.

Investing in Sustainability and Quality

When planning your home salon, consider the long-term value of your investment. Opting for durable, high-quality materials and equipment not only enhances the aesthetic but also reduces the need for frequent replacements. Furthermore, integrating sustainable practices can lead to long-term savings and align with a conscious lifestyle. You might consider materials that are locally sourced or have a lower environmental impact. Frequently Asked Questions about Home Salon Setup Costs

What is the minimum space required for a home salon?

A functional home salon can be set up in as little as 8x10 feet for basic services. However, for a more comprehensive setup with a relaxation area, 10x12 feet or larger is recommended. The key is efficient space planning and multi-functional furniture.

Can I do a home salon setup on a budget of INR 2,00,000?

Setting up a truly functional and hygienic home salon with professional-grade equipment for INR 2,00,000 would be challenging in 2025. You might be able to achieve a very basic setup with consumer-grade equipment and minimal renovation, focusing on essential services like hair styling or basic manicures. However, for a luxurious or professional experience, a higher budget is advisable.

How much does professional salon equipment cost in India?

Professional salon equipment can range from INR 50,000 for a basic set of tools and appliances to INR 3,00,000 or more for high-end, specialized machinery like facial steamers, high-frequency machines, and professional hair steamers.

Are there any hidden costs I should be aware of?

Yes, potential hidden costs can include permits (if required by local authorities), professional installation fees for certain equipment, ongoing maintenance, and the cost of consumables like beauty products, towels, and sanitizers.

How can I make my home salon look luxurious without a huge budget?

Focus on high-impact, low-cost elements: strategic use of mirrors to enhance space, good quality lighting, a calming colour palette, luxurious textiles like velvet cushions or soft throws, and high-quality scents. Decluttering and impeccable organisation also contribute significantly to a sense of luxury.
