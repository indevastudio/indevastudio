Home Salon Setup Cost India 2025: Luxury Design Guide

13 April 2026 · By Indéva Studio

Home Salon Setup Cost India 2025: Designing Your Personal Sanctuary

Imagine stepping into your own meticulously designed personal salon, right within your Delhi NCR home. The aroma of high-quality products, the soft glow of ambient lighting, and the feeling of ultimate relaxation as you pamper yourself. For many, this dream is within reach, but the question often arises: what is the actual home salon setup cost in India for 2025? At Indéva Studio, we understand that creating such a space involves more than just a chair and a mirror; it's about crafting an experience. We guide discerning homeowners through the nuances of designing spaces that blend luxury, functionality, and personal style, ensuring your investment reflects your aspirations.

Understanding the Investment: Factors Influencing Home Salon Costs

The cost of setting up a home salon in India in 2025 is not a one-size-fits-all figure. It's a spectrum influenced by several key elements. Your vision, the size of the dedicated space, the quality of furnishings and equipment, and the extent of customisation all play a significant role. Think of it as building a bespoke piece of furniture versus buying off the shelf; the former, while more expensive, offers unparalleled quality and a perfect fit.

Space Allocation and Preparation

The first step is identifying the right space. This could be a spare bedroom, a dedicated corner of your living room, or even a converted balcony. The costs here include:

  • Renovation/Modification: If walls need to be moved, new partitions created, or plumbing and electrical work done, this can range from ₹50,000 to ₹2,00,000+ depending on the complexity. For a high-end spa-like feel, consider features like a small steam shower or a dedicated wash basin area.
  • Flooring: Opting for premium, easy-to-clean, and water-resistant flooring like high-quality vitrified tiles or engineered wood can cost between ₹150-₹800 per square foot. For a luxurious touch, consider marble or granite accents, which can add ₹1,000-₹3,000 per square foot.
  • Wall Finishes: Beyond paint, consider textured finishes, designer wallpaper, or even marble cladding for a truly opulent look. These can add ₹50-₹300+ per square foot.

Essential Furniture and Fixtures

The core of your salon is its furniture. Investing in quality pieces ensures comfort, durability, and a sophisticated aesthetic.

  • Styling Chairs: For hair services, a comfortable, adjustable, and stylish hydraulic styling chair can range from ₹15,000 to ₹75,000 each.
  • Wash Units: A comfortable backwash unit with a good faucet is essential for hair services. These typically cost between ₹20,000 and ₹60,000.
  • Treatment Beds/Recliners: For facials, massages, and other beauty treatments, a sturdy and comfortable adjustable bed or recliner is crucial. Expect to spend ₹25,000 to ₹80,000.
  • Storage Units: Custom-built cabinets and shelves for storing products, towels, and equipment are vital for an organised space. Depending on the material and design, these can range from ₹30,000 to ₹1,50,000.
  • Mirrors: Beyond functional mirrors, consider decorative or backlit mirrors to enhance the ambiance. A large, well-designed mirror can cost ₹10,000 to ₹50,000.

The Heart of the Salon: Equipment and Technology

Beyond the furniture, the right equipment elevates your home salon from a hobby space to a professional-grade sanctuary. The cost here can vary significantly based on the specific treatments you intend to offer.

Hair Care and Styling Equipment

If hair is your focus, consider:

  • Professional Hair Dryers & Styling Tools: High-quality, durable hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons from reputable brands can cost ₹5,000 to ₹25,000.
  • Steamers and Hood Dryers: For advanced hair treatments and styling, a professional steamer can range from ₹15,000 to ₹40,000, and a hood dryer from ₹10,000 to ₹30,000.

Skincare and Beauty Equipment

For facials and other beauty treatments:

  • Facial Machines: Multi-functional facial machines offering functions like high-frequency, vacuum, spray, and galvanic currents can range from ₹20,000 to ₹70,000.
  • Magnifying Lamps: Essential for detailed work, these can cost ₹3,000 to ₹15,000.
  • Sterilisation Equipment: Maintaining hygiene is paramount. A good steriliser can cost ₹5,000 to ₹20,000.

Manicure and Pedicure Stations

If you plan to offer nail services:

  • Manicure Tables: Ergonomic and stylish manicure tables can range from ₹8,000 to ₹30,000.
  • Pedicure Stations: These can be simple chairs or elaborate units with built-in basins, costing ₹20,000 to ₹1,00,000+.
  • UV/LED Nail Lamps: For gel manicures, these are essential and cost ₹2,000 to ₹8,000.

Ambiance and Aesthetics: The Indéva Studio Touch

At Indéva Studio, we believe the soul of a luxury space lies in its ambiance. This is where thoughtful design elevates a functional area into a truly captivating experience. The cost for these elements contributes significantly to the overall luxury feel.

Lighting Design

Strategic lighting is crucial for both functionality and mood. We consider a layered approach:

  • Ambient Lighting: Recessed ceiling lights or elegant chandeliers to provide overall illumination. Costs can range from ₹10,000 to ₹50,000+.
  • Task Lighting: Focused lighting for specific workstations, such as adjustable spotlights or vanity lights. ₹5,000 to ₹20,000.
  • Accent Lighting: To highlight decorative elements or create a specific mood, like LED strip lights or wall sconces. ₹5,000 to ₹25,000.

Décor and Finishes

This is where your personal style shines through. Consider:

  • Colour Palette: Calming neutrals, rich jewel tones, or serene pastels can all contribute to a luxurious feel.
  • Textiles: Plush towels, comfortable seating cushions, and elegant drapes in high-quality fabrics.
  • Greenery: Indoor plants can add a touch of freshness and tranquility.
  • Artwork and Accents: Curated pieces that reflect your taste and the overall theme.

The investment in these decorative elements can vary widely, from ₹20,000 for a curated selection to ₹1,00,000+ for bespoke art and premium soft furnishings.

Sound and Scent

These often-overlooked elements can transform the atmosphere. Consider a high-quality Bluetooth speaker system (₹5,000-₹20,000) and investing in premium essential oils or diffusers (₹2,000-₹10,000).

Estimated Total Home Salon Setup Cost India 2025

Based on the factors above, here are some estimated cost ranges for a home salon setup in India for 2025, assuming a moderately sized space (approx. 150-200 sq ft) with premium finishes and good quality equipment:

  • Basic Setup (Functional & Neat): ₹2,00,000 - ₹4,00,000. This would include essential furniture, basic equipment, standard lighting, and paint finishes.
  • Mid-Range Setup (Comfortable & Stylish): ₹4,00,000 - ₹7,00,000. This range allows for better quality furniture, a wider range of equipment, improved lighting design, and more sophisticated finishes.
  • Luxury Setup (Opulent & Bespoke): ₹7,00,000 - ₹15,00,000+. This is for a truly high-end experience, featuring custom-designed furniture, top-tier professional equipment, advanced lighting and sound systems, premium materials, and expert interior design consultation from firms like Indéva Studio.

These figures are estimates and can be adjusted based on your specific needs and preferences. We encourage you to explore our services to understand how we can tailor a solution for your unique vision.

Frequently Asked Questions About Home Salon Setups

What is the average cost of a professional salon chair in India for 2025?

A good quality, hydraulic styling chair can range from ₹15,000 to ₹75,000 in India for 2025. Luxury brands or chairs with advanced features will be at the higher end of this spectrum.

Do I need special permits for a home salon in India?

Generally, for personal use, you do not require specific permits. However, if you plan to offer services to the public and run it as a business, you may need to check with your local municipal corporation regarding any licensing or zoning regulations.

How much should I budget for lighting in a home salon?

For a well-lit and atmospheric home salon, budget between ₹20,000 to ₹1,00,000+. This includes ambient, task, and accent lighting, with costs varying based on the fixtures and complexity of the design.

What are the most important pieces of equipment for a small home salon?

For a small home salon, prioritize a comfortable styling chair, a reliable hair dryer, basic styling tools, good lighting, and essential skincare equipment if offering facials. Storage solutions are also crucial for maintaining an organised space.

Where can I find inspiration for luxury home salon designs in India?

Inspiration can be found in many places. High-end interior design magazines, Pinterest, and websites like houzz india offer a wealth of ideas. Exploring our design projects will also give you a glimpse into the luxurious spaces we create.
