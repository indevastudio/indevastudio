Luxury Home Interior Design in India

By indéva Studio, Published on April 07, 2026

Introduction to Luxury Interior Design

Luxury home interior design in India is a rapidly growing field, with an increasing number of homeowners seeking to create unique and sophisticated living spaces. At indéva Studio, we specialize in providing bespoke interior design services that cater to the needs of discerning clients.

Understanding the Concept of Luxury

Luxury is often associated with high-end materials, exquisite craftsmanship, and attention to detail. In the context of interior design, luxury refers to the creation of spaces that exude elegance, sophistication, and refinement. Our team of expert interior designers at indéva Studio work closely with clients to understand their vision and preferences, and then translate those into reality.

The Importance of Space Planning

Space planning is a critical aspect of luxury interior design. It involves the careful consideration of the layout, proportions, and functionality of a space to create a harmonious and efficient environment. Our designers take into account factors such as the flow of natural light, the placement of furniture, and the selection of materials to create a cohesive and balanced design.

Indian Interior Design Elements

Indian interior design is known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse influences. From the intricate carvings of Mughal architecture to the vibrant colors of Indian textiles, there are countless elements that can be incorporated into a luxury interior design scheme. Our designers are well-versed in the principles of Vastu Shastra, an ancient Indian philosophy that emphasizes the importance of balance and harmony in design.

Luxury Home Decor and Furniture

Luxury home decor and furniture are essential components of a well-designed interior space. At indéva Studio, we offer a range of bespoke furniture design services, from custom-built cabinets to handcrafted decorative pieces. Our team of skilled craftsmen and designers work together to create unique and functional pieces that reflect the client's personal style and aesthetic.

Cost of Luxury Interior Design in India

The cost of luxury interior design in India can vary widely, depending on factors such as the size of the space, the materials and finishes used, and the level of complexity involved. On average, the cost of luxury interior design services in India can range from ₹5 lakh to ₹50 lakh or more, depending on the scope of the project.
