Vastu Compliant Home Design Principles

By indéva Studio | April 07, 2026

Introduction to Vastu Shastra

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science that deals with the design and construction of buildings, including homes. It is based on the idea that the physical environment around us affects our well-being and happiness. In this article, we will explore the principles of vastu compliant home design and how it can be applied to create a harmonious and balanced living space.

What is Vastu Compliant Design?

Vastu compliant design is a design approach that takes into account the principles of Vastu Shastra. It involves designing a home in a way that aligns with the natural energies and forces of the universe. This includes the layout of the home, the placement of rooms and furniture, and the use of colors and materials.

Benefits of Vastu Compliant Design

There are many benefits to designing a home that is vastu compliant. Some of the benefits include:

  • Improved health and well-being
  • Increased prosperity and wealth
  • Enhanced relationships and communication
  • Reduced stress and anxiety

How to Apply Vastu Compliant Design Principles

To apply vastu compliant design principles, it is essential to understand the basic principles of Vastu Shastra. This includes understanding the concept of the five elements (earth, water, fire, air, and space) and how they relate to the design of a home. It is also essential to consider the direction of the home, the layout of the rooms, and the placement of furniture and decor.

Vastu Tips for Home Design

Here are some vastu tips for home design:

  • Place the main entrance of the home in the north or east direction
  • Place the bedroom in the southwest direction
  • Place the kitchen in the southeast direction
  • Use colors and materials that are natural and earthy

Vastu and Interior Design

Vastu and interior design go hand-in-hand. A well-designed interior space can enhance the vastu compliance of a home. This includes the use of colors, textures, and patterns that are in harmony with the natural energies of the home.
