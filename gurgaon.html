common questions · gurgaon

before you start a project.

do you work in dlf the magnolias, aralias or camellias?

Yes. We work across DLF Phase 1–5 including The Magnolias, Aralias, Camellias, Belaire, Park Place, and The Crest. We manage society approval processes in all of these.

what areas in gurgaon do you cover?

All of Gurgaon — DLF Phases 1–5, Golf Course Road, Golf Course Extension, Sohna Road, MG Road, Cyber City, Sector 56–70, Nirvana Country, and Sushant Lok.

how much does luxury interior design cost in gurgaon?

Full-scope work starts at ₹2,250/sqft. Premium projects range from ₹3,750 to ₹7,000+ per sqft.

do you handle society approvals?

Yes — we manage society NOCs, RWA coordination, and work-window scheduling for all DLF and high-rise societies.

what is the timeline for a gurgaon high-rise apartment?

Design phase: 6–10 weeks. Execution for 2,500–4,000 sqft: 5–8 months.
