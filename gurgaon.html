questions clients ask · gurgaon

before you start a project.

do you work in dlf the magnolias, aralias, or camellias?

Yes. We work across DLF Phase 1 through 5 — including The Magnolias, Aralias, Camellias, Belaire, Park Place, The Crest, and Pinnacle. We are familiar with society approval processes in all of these.

what areas in gurgaon do you cover?

All of Gurgaon — DLF Phases 1–5, Golf Course Road, Golf Course Extension Road, Sohna Road, MG Road, Cyber City, Sector 56 to 70, Nirvana Country, and Sushant Lok.

how much does luxury interior design cost in gurgaon?

For high-rise apartments, full-scope work starts at ₹4,500/sqft. Premium projects with custom joinery and imported stone range from ₹7,000 to ₹14,000+ per sqft.

do you handle society approvals and coordination?

Yes. For high-rise work in DLF and other gated communities, we manage society approvals, NOC applications, work-window scheduling, and RWA coordination.

what is the timeline for a gurgaon high-rise apartment?

Design phase is 6–10 weeks. Execution for a 2,500–4,000 sqft apartment is typically 5–8 months, depending on custom joinery and society work-window restrictions.
